FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Do I need to change my Stripe integration?
Nope. SimpleRecover connects via Stripe Connect (OAuth) and uses webhooks. Zero code changes. Zero downtime. Your existing billing flow stays exactly the same.
What happens after the 14-day trial?
We ask for a credit card and you start paying $19/mo. If you choose not to continue, we simply stop retrying and sending emails. No data lock-in. You can export everything.
Does this work with PayPal, Paddle, or Lemon Squeezy?
Currently Stripe only. We're evaluating Paddle and Lemon Squeezy support for Q3 2026. PayPal is unlikely due to their limited webhook capabilities for dunning.
Can I customize the email copy?
Yes. Every email in the sequence is editable. We provide templates that work out of the box, but you can rewrite them to match your brand voice. We also support custom sender names and reply-to addresses.
Is my customer data safe?
We never store full card numbers — Stripe handles all payment data. We store email addresses and payment metadata (last 4 digits, expiry date) encrypted at rest. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant.
