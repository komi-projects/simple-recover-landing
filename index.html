FAQ

Questions? Answers.

How does SimpleRecover connect to Stripe? +

SimpleRecover uses Stripe webhooks and a restricted API key. You create a webhook endpoint pointing to SimpleRecover, and provide a read-only API key. We never charge your customers or modify your Stripe data — we only read payment events and trigger retries through Stripe's API.

How long does setup take? +

Average setup time is 10 minutes. Connect your Stripe account, configure your dunning email templates, and set your retry schedule. No code changes to your app required.

What payment methods do you support? +

Currently Stripe (credit/debit cards). PayPal support is on our Q3 roadmap. We optimize retry logic based on card type, failure reason, and customer behavior.

Can I customize the dunning emails? +

Yes! Starter plan includes our proven 5-email sequence. Pro and Enterprise plans include a drag-and-drop email builder where you can customize branding, tone, timing, and content.

Is my customer data secure? +

Absolutely. We use restricted API keys with read-only access. Webhook signatures are verified. We don't store full card numbers. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

What if I want to cancel? +

Cancel anytime. No contracts, no questions. Your data is exportable. If you're on annual billing, we'll prorate the refund. We win when you win — if you're not recovering revenue, we don't deserve your business.

Do you offer annual billing? +

Yes! Annual billing includes 2 months free (17% discount). Contact us for enterprise annual contracts with additional custom terms.

Can I see a demo before signing up? +

Of course! Start a free 14-day trial — no credit card required. You'll see the full dashboard, recovery analytics, and email previews. Or book a 15-minute demo with our team.
